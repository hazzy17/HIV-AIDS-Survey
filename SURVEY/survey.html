HIV/AIDS awareness is crucial for creating a healthier, stigma-free world. By educating communities, we can prevent new infections, support those affected, and encourage regular testing. Empowering people with knowledge fosters compassion and resilience, helping to reduce transmission rates and ensure everyone has access to treatment and care

An HIV survey helps assess awareness, identify high-risk behaviors, and evaluate the impact of prevention programs. It provides vital data for improving healthcare strategies, reducing infection rates, and supporting those affected by HIV/AIDS.
